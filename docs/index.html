Rationale

The Learning Commons Center library is multifaceted. It serves as the heart of the academic community, providing access to a vast array of scholarly resources, including print and electronic books, academic journals, research, and multimedia materials. Moreover, the library's role extends beyond just providing resources; it is a place where knowledge is brought together with learners.

Mission

To be a vibrant and innovative learning hub that empowers library users to excel in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Vision

To foster a collaborative and inclusive learning environment that promotes critical thinking, information literacy, and lifelong learning through access to diverse resources, cutting-edge technologies, and personalized support.

Goal

Empowering learners to become information-literate global citizens, champion academic success, cultivate innovation and creativity, embrace technological advancements, and champion open access and digital scholarship.

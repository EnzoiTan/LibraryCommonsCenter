LIBRARY SECTION

Reference Section

This section contains ready-references on general knowledge and relevant sources facts such as encyclopedias. atlases dictionaries, handbooks, manual, etc.

Reserve Section

This section contains in-demand books with limited copies currently used by the faculty members.

Circulation Section

This section contains multiple copies of books that can be used by students and faculty freely on the open shelves and can be borrowed for overnight and home reading.

Periodical Section

This section contains periodicals such as magazines, journals, newspapers, pamphlets, leaflets, etc. All materials are for librayr use only.

Filipiniana Section

This section contains books written by a Filipino author, printed and published in the Philippines, or books about the Philippines.

Maritime Section

This section contains books about maritime education. This collection can be borrowed for overnight and home reading
